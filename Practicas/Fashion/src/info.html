FASHION SHOWS

High-class fashion show concept, choreography and live show directing. The fashion shows are tailored to the needs of clients: collection presentation, fashion week or different kind of fashion event. The mood is created with sound, visuals, lighting and runway choreography, with the emphasis on presenting a show.
